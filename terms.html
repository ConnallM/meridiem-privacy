4. What Meridiem is — and is not

Meridiem estimates cumulative UV exposure by combining Apple Watch daylight data with the UV index for your location, measured against a personalised limit derived from your skin type. These are estimates. Real-world sunburn depends on many factors Meridiem cannot fully observe — clothing, shade, sunscreen application quality, reflective surfaces, individual variation, and sensor and forecast accuracy.
